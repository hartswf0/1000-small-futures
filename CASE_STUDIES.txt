🐉 → 👤 (knight restored)
⚔ (broken) + 💔 (guilt) → ✦ (redemption)

 failures are doorways, not endings. Thank you for breaking it twice."

│ strength. Failure becomes foundation.          │

LEARNED: Branching, symbolic depth, inversion of tropes
